Instruments Of Change


Our Mission:

Here at Instruments Of Change we believe music can not only make the world a better place but create
opportunities in a child's life that might not have been availble to them otherwise. Our primary focus is
providing musical instruments and instruction to under-privileged children and teens. We do this via
charitable donations; we accept donations from both businesses and private people. We also accept
volunteers to help with either our events or assisting in the musical education of the children.
